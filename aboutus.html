Who Are We?


Astournauta is a company that provides individuals with the chance to explore space by becoming an astronaut. Our mission is to provide training, knowledge, certifications, and technology alongside any other skills needed for one to be classified as an astronaut. We believe that space exploration is the next steps in human advancements and want to provide accessible methods to be a part of such a monumental achievement. With inspiration from our favorite artist in respect to the name of our company. Astournauta aims to cultivate astronauts that are ready to explore the galaxy with the services we provide them.
